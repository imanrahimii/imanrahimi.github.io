# Industry Profile

## Overview

I am a **senior Data Scientist and Decision Scientist** specialising in the delivery of **machine learning and analytics solutions** that support operational and strategic decision-making.

My industry work focuses on **turning data and models into deployable decision tools**, rather than isolated analyses.

---

## What I Deliver

- End-to-end ML solutions: data → model → decision support  
- Predictive analytics and forecasting for planning  
- Decision intelligence systems under real-world constraints  
- Scenario analysis and trade-off exploration  
- Explainable, stakeholder-ready analytics

## How I Work

- Strong problem formulation aligned with business objectives  
- Practical model selection focused on robustness and interpretability  
- Clear communication with technical and non-technical stakeholders  
- Emphasis on scalability, maintainability, and decision relevance
