## Selected Applied Research & Industry Projects

**Large-Scale Decision Support for Open-Pit Mining**  
Design and development of decision support systems for long-term open-pit mine scheduling under geological uncertainty. This work integrates evolutionary optimisation, decomposition techniques, and GPU-accelerated computation to handle extremely large problem sizes while maintaining solution quality and feasibility. The focus is on generating actionable schedules that can be used in real planning environments rather than purely theoretical benchmarks.

**GPU-Accelerated Optimisation and High-Performance Algorithms**  
Development of performance-critical optimisation and numerical algorithms using GPU and parallel architectures. This includes optimising memory access patterns, numerical stability, and computational throughput for large-scale optimisation and analytical workloads, with direct relevance to time-sensitive industrial decision processes.

**Energy Systems Analytics and Optimisation**  
Application of machine learning, surrogate modelling, and multi-objective optimisation to energy forecasting, planning, and sustainability-related decision problems. This work supports decision-makers in evaluating trade-offs between cost, reliability, emissions, and operational constraints.

**Hybrid ML‚ÄìOptimisation Frameworks for Decision Intelligence**  
Design of integrated frameworks that combine predictive models (e.g., forecasting, surrogate models) with optimisation engines to enable adaptive, data-driven decision-making. These frameworks are designed to operate in dynamic environments where data evolves over time and decisions must be updated continuously.
